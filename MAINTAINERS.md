# Maintainers

Rekoll is maintained by named, accountable people — no pseudonyms (see
[DESIGN.md](docs/DESIGN.md) §9, the anti-MemPalace-pseudonym rule).

| Maintainer | GitHub | Role | Areas |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ryan Kyle (Rekreate Digital) | [@ryankyleocampo-github](https://github.com/ryankyleocampo-github) | Lead maintainer | Everything |

## Contact

- **General:** open a [Discussion](https://github.com/ryankyleocampo-github/rekoll/discussions) or an issue.
- **Security:** never public — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for private reporting.
- **Code of Conduct:** conduct@rekreatedigital.com

## How decisions are made

- Load-bearing decisions are recorded as ADRs under [`docs/adr/`](docs/adr/).
- Changes that conflict with the [non-goals](NON_GOALS.md) are declined regardless
  of quality — that's a feature, not gatekeeping.
- This is a small project; **response times vary**. That's expected and okay.

## Becoming a maintainer

Sustained, high-quality contributions that respect the non-goals can earn triage
and commit rights over time. The path is simple: keep showing up with good PRs and
reviews, then start a conversation in Discussions.
